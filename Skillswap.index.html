Frequently Asked Questions

Skillswap operates on a barter system where members exchange skills with one another instead of money. You can list the skills you can teach and the skills you want to learn, and our platform helps you find compatible partners.

No, Skillswap is a completely free platform. Our goal is to promote learning and skill exchange without financial barriers.

You can learn a wide variety of skills, from web development and graphic design to photography, cooking, and languages. The skills available depend on what our members are willing to teach.

Our platform includes a matching feature that connects you with other members who have skills you want to learn and are interested in the skills you can teach. You can also browse through member profiles and contact them directly.
